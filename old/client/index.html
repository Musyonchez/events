Student Support Services

USIU is committed to empowering student activities and campus life through a comprehensive suite of services that foster growth, engagement, and success. Our support covers everything from event planning and club management to holistic student well-being.

Event Planning Assistance

Guidance and resources for clubs and departments to organize, promote, and manage campus events with ease and professionalism.

Club & Society Management

Dedicated support for club registration, leadership training, budgeting, and activity reporting to ensure vibrant student organizations.

Counseling & Wellness

Confidential counseling, mental health support, and wellness workshops to help students thrive personally and academically.

Career & Leadership Development

Career counseling, internship placement, leadership training, and networking events to prepare students for professional success.

Sports & Recreation

Access to sports facilities, leagues, and fitness programs to promote teamwork, health, and campus spirit.

Accommodation & Welfare

Guidance on housing, scholarships, and welfare programs to support every aspect of student life on campus.
