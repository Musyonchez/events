Frequently Asked Questions

You can join any club by attending their meetings or events. Most clubs welcome new members throughout the year. Check the clubs listing page for contact details and upcoming activities.

Clubs organize a variety of activities including workshops, competitions, cultural festivals, sports tournaments, community service, and social events to enrich your campus life.

To start a new club, gather interested students and draft a club constitution. Submit your proposal to the Student Affairs Office for approval. Once approved, you can officially register and begin activities.

Most clubs do not charge membership fees. However, some may request contributions for special events or activities. Check with the specific club for details.
